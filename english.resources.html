Recommended books written in English

  • LinguaMarina Main focus on daily, conversation American English + tip and tricks how to learn a language faster

  • Rachel's English Main focus on American English

  • English with Lucy Main focus on British English

  • Love English with Leila & Sabrah Main focus on British English

  • How not to die - Based on the latest scientific research, the book examines each of the most common diseases to reveal what, how and why different foods affect us. It also shares Dr Greger's 'Daily Dozen' - the twelve foods we should all eat every day to stay in the best of health.

  • Atomic Habits - the definitive guide to breaking bad behaviors and adopting good ones in four steps, showing you how small, incremental, everyday routines compound into massive, positive change over time

  • The courage of being disliked - “The courage to be happy also includes the courage to be disliked. When you have gained that courage, your interpersonal relationships will all at once change into things of lightness.” Direct quote from the book.
