Apps

  • Mein Weg nach Deutschland Beginner-friendly study of Nevin Coscun moving to Germany. Every episode provides subtitles and lasts 5-7 minutes.

  • Learn German Various lessons in German, including conversational german, german grammar and social customs in Germany.

  • Deutsch lernen mit der DWn Similar to Learn German, various lessons in German

  • Speakly - for intermediate lerners, learn phases, vocab in context.

  • Clozemaster - very similar to the speakly, but for free.

  • Speekoo - learn useful phases, and vocab in context.

  • Easy German News - TODAI - for intermediate lerners, wold-wide news in german.
